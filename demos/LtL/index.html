Neighbor count is over the (2R+1)² box — or, with circle checked, over cells with dx²+dy² ≤ R² (counted by FFT convolution; R may be half-integer, Game of Life = R 1.5) — and includes the cell itself (Evans' convention). Toggling circle switches an unmodified preset to its disk equivalent and rescales a custom rule to the new neighborhood area. Drag: left = draw, right = erase. Ctrl+drag: select a region and save it as a creature file (zero borders trimmed). Load or drop a creature JSON onto the canvas, then click to paste; Esc / right-click stops. Wheel: zoom. Shift+drag or middle-drag: pan. G: toggle grid lines.
